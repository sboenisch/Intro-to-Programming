Cheetah

Cheetah is the fastest land animal, capable of running at 80 to 128 km/h, and as such has several adaptations for speed, including a light build, long thin legs and a long tail

  • Scientific name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Average Length (Shoulder): 67-94 cm
  • Average Length (Head-and-Body): 1.1-1.5 m
  • Average Weight: 20-65 kg
  • Habitat: Savanna

These cats are lean and lanky, allowing them to reach incredible speeds. They have tan-colored fur with solid black, round spots. They have very long legs, a long narrow tail for balance, and a deep chest that contains large lungs
